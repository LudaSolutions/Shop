Shipping is calculated separately for orders of 99 pieces or less. Free shipping is available for orders of 100 or more bags.

USA orders

Prices include 10% import duty, which is paid at dispatch. If any additional charges apply, they are the buyer's responsibility.

Europe and other destinations

Customs duties, import taxes and local handling fees are paid by the buyer unless agreed otherwise in the quote.
